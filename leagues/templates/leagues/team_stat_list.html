How Standings Are Calculated
Points: Win=3  •  OTW=2  •  OTL/Tie=1  •  Loss=0
Tiebreakers (in order):
  1. Total Points
  2. Regulation Wins (excludes OT wins)
  3. Head-to-Head Record
  4. Goal Differential (GF − GA)
GP=Games Played  •  GF=Goals For  •  GA=Goals Against  •  DIFF=Goal Differential
